Small builds that give salvaged parts a practical second life. Each project lists the parts it needs and walks through construction from scratch.

## Bench tools

[Continuity Tester](01-continuity-tester.html) — A salvaged LED, a resistor, and a small battery cell. One of the fastest ways to turn scrap into something genuinely useful at the bench.

[Capacitor Discharge Tool](02-cap-discharge-tool.html) — A resistor and a pair of probes for safely draining capacitors before working on mains-powered boards.

[ATX Bench Supply](03-atx-bench-supply.html) — Converts a scrap ATX power supply into a regulated bench supply with 3.3 V, 5 V, and 12 V rails.

[Component Tester Jig](04-component-tester-jig.html) — A ZIF-socket breakout board for quickly measuring components without soldering them in.

## Power and lighting

[USB Charger](05-usb-charger.html) — A 5 V USB charging output built from a salvaged switching regulator and a wall-charger enclosure.

[LED Lamp](06-led-lamp.html) — A low-power task lamp built from salvaged LED strips, a driver board, and a repurposed enclosure.
