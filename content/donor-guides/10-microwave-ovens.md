This guide will cover microwave ovens as a donor class with genuinely useful motors, switches, fans, and hardware, but also with one of the clearest cases in the guide where an untrained reader should usually stop and walk away.

## TODO: How to Get Them Out
Explain that low-risk salvage focuses on mechanical parts accessed away from the high-voltage section, and that any plan requiring movement near the capacitor or transformer belongs only in procedures written by trained authors.

## TODO: Watch Out For
State plainly that the high-voltage capacitor can remain lethal long after unplugging, that the magnetron and HV transformer area are not beginner territory, and that many readers should skip microwave teardown entirely.
